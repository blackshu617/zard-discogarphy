BIOGRAPHY
ZARD（ザード）is a musical unit centered around vocalist Izumi Sakai（坂井泉水）.
She began playing the piano at the age of six. After graduating from university, she spent some time working under a modeling agency. Later, she met music producer Daiko Nagato, who recognized her exceptional talent as an artist, leading to the formation of ZARD.
The unit made its debut on February 10, 1991, with the single "Good-bye My Loneliness." To date, they have released 45 singles and 22 albums. Boasting staggering sales of 17.733 million singles and 19.998 million albums, ZARD ranked 8th in Oricon's Top Artist Sales for the Heisei Era. Beyond writing for ZARD, Sakai also provided lyrics for numerous other artists, many of which became million-selling hits.
Even after Izumi Sakai's passing on May 27, 2007, her music continues to be listened to and sung by countless people, enduring as "eternal standard numbers." (source: wezard.net)

ZARD（ザード）was a Japanese pop rock group. Originally a group of five members, with lead vocalist Izumi Sakai（坂井泉水）as group leader. However, Izumi Sakai was the only member who stayed on in the group while others joined and left regularly.
